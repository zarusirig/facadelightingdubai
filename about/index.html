About Facade Lighting Dubai

Engineering-led facade lighting specialists. We design, specify, install, and maintain regulation-compliant, climate-adapted facade lighting systems for buildings across Dubai and the UAE.

Our Mission

Facade Lighting Dubai exists to bridge the gap between architectural vision and engineering reality in one of the world's most demanding built environments. Dubai's extreme climate — sustained temperatures above 45°C, coastal salt spray, UV exposure exceeding 2,000 kWh/m² annually, and periodic sandstorms — destroys lighting systems that perform adequately elsewhere. Our mission is to ensure every facade lighting installation we touch is designed for these conditions from day one.

We combine deep knowledge of Dubai's regulatory framework — Al Sa'fat Green Building Evaluation System, DEWA electrical standards, Dubai Civil Defence NOC requirements, and master developer compliance codes — with proven engineering methodology to deliver facade lighting that performs, complies, and endures.

What We Do

Our work spans the full lifecycle of facade lighting projects:

Design & Specification

We develop photometric designs, colour temperature strategies, layered lighting techniques, and full technical specifications tailored to each building's facade material, orientation, and regulatory zone. Every design is backed by photometric simulation reporting and wind load calculations.

Installation & Commissioning

From structural assessment through electrical infrastructure to final commissioning and handover, we manage every phase. Our high-rise installation protocols and safety procedures meet international standards adapted for Dubai's construction environment.

Maintenance & Support

Dubai's environment is relentless. Our annual inspection programmes, cleaning schedules, and DMX troubleshooting protocols keep systems performing at design intent year after year. We address LED driver failures and component degradation before they become visible problems.
