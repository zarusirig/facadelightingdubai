Best Facade Lighting Brands Available in the UAE

The brand you choose for facade lighting determines three things: fixture longevity in Dubai's climate, spare parts availability, and warranty support when problems occur. This guide categorises the brands available in the UAE market by tier, with honest assessment of their suitability for exterior applications in extreme heat, humidity, and salt spray conditions.

Brand comparison

Selection criteria for Dubai

  • Housing material: 316L stainless steel or marine-grade aluminium for coastal locations. Die-cast aluminium acceptable inland.
  • IP rating: Minimum IP66 for facade fixtures. IP68 for recessed ground installations. See Dubai-grade specification.
  • Driver quality: Tc-rated electrolytic capacitors suitable for Dubai's ambient. See driver failure guide.
  • UAE stock: Brands with local stock can deliver in 1-2 weeks. Import-only brands take 8-12 weeks — critical for project timelines.
  • Warranty terms: Verify warranty covers UAE operating conditions (some warranties void above 40°C ambient).
