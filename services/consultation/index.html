Facade Lighting Consultation Dubai: Expert Project Guidance

Our facade lighting consultation service provides independent, expert guidance through every project stage — from initial design concept through Dubai Grade specification, regulatory navigation, procurement support, installation supervision, and commissioning. We work with property developers, architects, interior designers, and building owners across all Dubai areas and building types.

What Our Consultation Covers

Technical Specification Dubai Grade fixture specification Climate-proven reliability

Who We Work With

  • Property developers — from concept design input through to building completion certificate.
  • Architects and interior designers — integrating facade lighting with architectural vision.
  • Building owners and FM teams — for retrofit, upgrade, and maintenance optimization.
  • Contractors — for technical specification support and commissioning expertise.

Our Process

  • Step 1: Discovery call. Free 30-minute consultation to understand your project, building type, location, timeline, and requirements.
  • Step 2: Site assessment. Visit the building (or review architectural drawings for new construction) to assess facade materials, orientation, access, and environmental conditions.
  • Step 3: Design proposal. Deliver a lighting concept with photometric simulations, fixture specification, control system recommendation, and budget estimate.
  • Step 4: Implementation support. Guide the project through procurement, installation, and commissioning to achieve the design intent.
